Historical Fact about the Team

Basketball Team

His real name is Wardell Stephen Curry II. He was born in Akron, Ohio on March 14, 1988. His parents are both former athletes. His father, Dell, played in the NBA and his mother, Sonya, played volleyball at Virginia Tech. He is a professional basketball player for the Golden State Warriors and the first person to be named Most Valuable Player by unanimous vote in NBA history. He holds the record for three-pointers in a season in both the NBA and NCAA. He is a gifted golfer and extremely active in charity work.

He is ambidextrous and can use both hands equally well. He was a talented football player in high school and was recruited by several colleges. He was involved in a controversy when his mother bought him a Hummer as a birthday gift before he entered the NBA draft. He was the first black man on the cover of Vogue magazine in 2008. He married his high school sweetheart, Savannah Brinson, in 2013. He was not the highest-paid player in the NBA until he reached 31 years old in 2016. He never met his biological father, who was an ex-con and left his mother when he was a baby. He is a supporter of former President Barack Obama and campaigned for him in 2008 and 2012. He has endorsements from several brands, such as Nike, Coca-Cola, Beats by Dre, and Blaze Pizza. He has stakes in Liverpool FC, an English soccer club, and has earned millions from their success.

One of the most distinctive features of Kawhi Leonard’s game is his enormous hands, which have earned him the nickname “The Claw.” But did you know that his hands didn’t always look like that? It was during his time in high school that Leonard’s hands began to develop their unique shape, with an exceptionally large palm and significantly long fingers. These unusual hands attribute to his incredible ability to steal the ball and make difficult shots.
